About Us

Secure technology solutions for any mission.

  • About


Global Commerce & Services, LLC (GCS) is an Information Technology company founded in 2000. The company has provided services in the areas of IT Solutions and Systems Engineering, Cybersecurity, Local Area Network/Wide Area Network (LAN/WAN) Development and Support, and Program/Project Management. Today, GCS is an SBA Certified HUBZone and Economically Disadvantaged Woman-Owned Small Business.

Our Experience

But what differentiates us is not our designations, but our accomplishments, awards, capabilities and commitment to meeting and exceeding the expectations on every Information Technology project. With deep experience in Information Technology, including both management and technical expertise, GCS has successfully completed projects for several DoD and other federal agencies, and won prestigious awards for our work.

Our Leadership

Our leadership provides hands-on oversight across all engagements, ensuring accountability, quality, and alignment with customer mission objectives. Most importantly, every project is constantly monitored and evaluated by senior management to ensure the results will meet or exceed contract requirements.
