GSA’s Multiple Award Schedule (MAS) is an Indefinite Delivery/ Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) Multiple Award Schedule contract is the largest, most widely used acquisition vehicle in the Federal Government. MAS – ‘Services Schedule delivers federal, state, and local customer agencies the tools and expertise needed to shorten procurement cycles, ensure compliance, and obtain the best value for innovative technology products, services, and solutions’. It is a procurement vehicle that offers a comprehensive array of state-of-the-art IT products, services, and solutions – including services such as: network services, cybersecurity, professional IT services, and more.

GCS has the IT Services SIN (SIN 54151S) under our GSA MAS enabling us to offer: • Cloud services (all associated labor) • Cognitive computing • Conversion and implementation support • Database planning and design • Internet of Things (IoT) • IT project management • Migration services (of all kinds) • Network services • Programming • Resources and facilities management • Systems analysis, design, and implementation

The GCS MAS contract has multiple labor categories to choose from when pricing opportunities under this contract vehicle. The benefits of using our MAS contract are the widespread use of modern IT paradigms help to ensure protection of data, increased administrative efficiencies, greater cost savings, and improved customer experience.
