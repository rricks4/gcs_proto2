Who We Are

Global Commerce & Services, LLC (GCS) is an Information Technology company founded in 2000.

GCS provides IT solutions, systems engineering, cybersecurity, LAN/WAN development and support, and program and project management services.

Mission-Focused Delivery

We focus on translating business needs into technology solutions. GCS partners with clients to provide value-added solutions that meet individual business requirements and help organizations capitalize on their technology investments.

Our clients include the Department of Agriculture, Department of Navy, U.S. Air Force, U.S. Coast Guard, Department of Army, U.S. Peace Corps, Defense Information Systems Agency (DISA), and NASA.
